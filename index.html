Naval Submarine Medical Research Lab Intern

Summer 2015

To enhance my engineering knowledge I spent my junior year summer in high school working for the Naval Submarine Medical Research Lab in New Longdon Connecticut. After working for the subbase, I was so excited by this project that I continued to do an independent study in Arduino and updated my poster to be able to send text messages depending on loudness.
